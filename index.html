About Us

Welcome to Flavour Wheels where culinary passion meets the open road! Founded in 2012, our food truck is more than just a mobile kitchen; it is a labor of love driven by a desire to bring delicious, high-quality food directly to our community.

Looking to add a unique touch to your next event? Our food truck is available for private parties, corporate events, weddings, and more. Contact us for more details on how we can help make your event deliciously memorable.

At Flavour Wheels, our mission is simple: to serve up mouth-watering, fresh, innovative and authentic food that brings joy to every customer. We are committed to:

  • Quality: Using only the freshest, locally-sourced ingredients.
  • Flavor: Crafting each dish with care and creativity to deliver bold, unforgettable flavors.
  • Community: Engaging with our community and supporting local events and causes.
  • Sustainability: Implementing eco-friendly practices in our operations, from using biodegradable
    • packaging to minimizing food waste.
